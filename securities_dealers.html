Business liability insurance services are one of our services. These services facilitate the parties for buying and selling of Securities and these securities belongs to their own accounts or to the third party accounts. These are for the purpose to resale either in short term purpose in the secondary market or in the primary market these securities publicly offer. (See also: Federal Act on Stock Exchanges and Securities Trading (SESTA). They include proprietary traders, issuing houses, derivative companies, market makers, and traders for clients.

In Germany and Switzerland some of the security dealer doesn’t have any bank status as banks are not allowed to interfere in their decisions because they work by their own independency as many of them are the members of Swiss Association of Independent Securities Dealers.
Financial Market Supervisory Authority (FINMA) facilitate business liability insurance services and helps the Broker-Dealers to trade their securities by giving registration, as many of the banks are also security dealers and the rest of the few are independent and don’t have bank status.
