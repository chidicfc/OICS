Introduction

Centrum Asset Managementis a global financial services firm specializing in private wealth management, institutional investments and investment banking services for private, institutional and corporate clients worldwide. Centrum Asset Managementwas founded in few years ago and has headquarters in Germany and offices in Switzerland. Centrum Asset Management operates under the regulation of the Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority (FINMA) and the German Securities Authority (GSA). As a boutique firm, we strive to consistently deliver exceptional service and superior performance for our clients. Our success derives from our core values: uncompromising professionalism, innovative thinking and a boutique, client-oriented approach.

What we offer?

  • Treasury Services
  • Investment Banking
  • Investment Management
  • Private Banking

We offer cash management, liquidity solutions, commercial cards and escrow services that help resolve the working capital and efficiency challenges treasury professionals face.

A global leader, we deliver strategic advice and solutions, including capital raising, risk management, and trade finance to corporations, institutions and governments.
We are a leader in investment management, dedicated to creating a strategic advantage for institutions by connecting clients with Centrum Asset Management investment professionals globally.

Why Us?

  • Expertise

    At Centrum Asset Management, asset management is our business. We're known for our investment expertise and customer focus -- we focus on our customers' needs. We take a predominantly long-only approach. We aim to grow our clients' assets based on fundamentally sound investments, and avoiding market fads. Our processes and portfolios are transparent to our clients - they trust in what we're doing because they understand it.

  • Our Values

    Our values are integral to the way we conduct our business and shape the experiences of our clients and employees. We value integrity, teamwork, innovation and performance. Integrity is the cornerstone of our business. Through global and local teams we innovate, and through our performance we deliver results.

  • Our Strategy

    We believe that the best way to grow the real purchasing power of our clients' assets is by owning quality, well positioned companies with both a domestic and global reach.
